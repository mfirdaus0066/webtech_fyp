Why We Created This Website

Many cats in our community face abandonment, neglect, or long stays in shelters with limited resources.

To help solve this, we created a dedicated cat adoption website that gathers and organizes data from various sources.

Our goal is to provide a wider, more efficient platform to showcase adoptable cats and simplify the adoption process.
