Why We Created This Website

Many cats in our community face the risk of abandonment, neglect, or long-term stays in shelters. These shelters often have limited resources and capacity, making it challenging to find suitable homes for all the cats in their care. A dedicated cat adoption website can help address this problem by providing a wider platform for showcasing adoptable cats and simplifying the adoption process. This report outlines the development of such a website, from the initial needs assessment to the final implementation, and discusses its potential to improve cat welfare. This is the reason we chose this title for our web project; these web technologies provide a wider platform for showcasing adoptable cats and simplifying the adoption process. Our project is focused on providing a wider platform for cat adoption, as we gather all the data from other websites and organize it on our site.
